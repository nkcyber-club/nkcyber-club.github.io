About Us

Welcome to NKCyber, the Cyber Security Club dedicated to promoting awareness and education about cybersecurity. Our club aims to create a safe and secure online environment for everyone.

Activities

We organize various activities, including workshops, seminars, and CTF (Capture The Flag) competitions, to help our members enhance their cyber skills and stay up-to-date with the latest trends in cybersecurity.

Join Us

If you are passionate about cybersecurity or want to learn more about it, we welcome you to join our club. Membership is open to all students, regardless of their background or experience in cybersecurity.
